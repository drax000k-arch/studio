# **App Name**: Advisify AI

## Core Features:

- Decision Input: Allow users to input a subject and a list of options to consider for decision-making.
- AI Decision Analysis: Use AI to analyze the options based on logical reasoning, budget considerations, and user-defined parameters.  The AI acts as a tool, determining how best to weigh the available data.
- Optimal Option Recommendation: Present the user with the AI's recommended optimal option, along with a justification for the choice.
- Community Option Posting: Enable users to post their decision scenarios and options to a public forum.
- Community Feedback: Allow other users to view, comment on, and provide feedback on posted decision scenarios.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: HSL(210, 60%, 50%) – A moderate blue (#4787D6) to convey trust and reliability, nodding to the app's decision-making assistance. The saturation evokes forward movement while being visually inviting.
- Background color: HSL(210, 20%, 95%) – A light blue tint (#F0F4F8) provides a clean and calming backdrop that doesn't distract from the AI recommendations.
- Accent color: HSL(180, 70%, 40%) – A teal color (#2DAB9A) adds a vibrant highlight, emphasizing crucial interactive elements and AI-driven suggestions.
- Body text and headlines: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif offering a modern look, balanced by a touch of warmth, ensuring readability and engagement.
- Use simple, flat icons to represent different decision categories (e.g., finance, travel, health). Each icon will change hue in accordance with its recommendation weighting in the ai decision analysis, indicating the decision weighting importance
- Maintain a clean and intuitive layout with clear sections for inputting options, displaying AI recommendations, and viewing community feedback.
- Use subtle animations to highlight the AI's decision-making process and provide feedback on user interactions.
